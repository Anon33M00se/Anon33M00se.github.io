How does this site work?

A few people have asked me about how I manage this site. It changes frequently, as I will often through stuff up very quickly, move it around, and take it down again.

The whole site controlled by editing local files in emacs and then a couple of quick keystrokes pushes out any changes to a free web service running on github. I'll explain each of the pieces in turn.
